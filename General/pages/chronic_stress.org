#+title: chronic stress
#+date: [2022-06-23 Thu 19:46]
#+filetags: :Health:

* Content
** When your body is in an almost constent state of stress
** Symptoms include: 
*** Anxiety
*** Depression
*** Digestion problems
*** Headaches
*** Muscle tension and pain
*** Heart disease
*** Sleep Problems
*** Weight gain
*** Memory impairment
