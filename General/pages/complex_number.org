#+title: complex number

* Content
** A complex number is an ordered pair \[ (a, b) \in \mathbb{R} \]  
** usually written in the form \[ a + b i \] 
** The set of all complex numbers is denoted by \[ \mathbb{C} = a + bi : a, b \in \mathbb{R}  \]
